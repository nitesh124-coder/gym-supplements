Weight Gain Diet Plan

A comprehensive diet plan designed to help you gain healthy weight and build muscle mass. This plan focuses on calorie surplus, high-quality proteins, and nutrient-dense foods.

Note: This is general guidance — for personalized results, consult a nutritionist or fitness professional.

Key Principles for Weight Gain

  • Calorie surplus (300-500 calories above maintenance)
  • High protein intake (1.6-2.2g per kg of bodyweight)
  • Complex carbohydrates (40-60% of total calories)
  • Healthy fats (20-30% of total calories)
  • 5-6 meals per day
  • Stay hydrated (3-4 liters of water daily)

Day 1 Sample Weight Gain Diet Plan

Meal 1 (Breakfast)
  • 4 whole eggs + 2 egg whites
  • 2 slices whole grain toast with peanut butter
  • 1 cup oatmeal with honey and banana
  • 1 glass whole milk
Meal 2 (Mid-Morning)
  • Mass gainer shake with milk
  • 1 banana
  • Handful of mixed nuts
Meal 3 (Lunch)
  • 200g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
Meal 4 (Pre-Workout)
  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 1 apple
  • 5-6 dates
Meal 5 (Post-Workout)
  • 2 scoops whey protein
  • 1 cup sweet potato
  • 1 banana
Meal 6 (Dinner)
  • 200g lean beef or fish
  • 1 cup quinoa or brown rice
  • Large mixed salad with olive oil
  • 1 avocado
