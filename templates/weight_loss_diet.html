Weight Loss Diet Plan

Here's a simple and effective weight loss diet plan designed to help reduce body fat while maintaining energy and preserving muscle. It's built around whole foods, portion control, and balanced macronutrients.

Note: This is general guidance — for personalized results, consult a nutritionist or fitness professional.

Key Principles for Weight Loss

  • Calorie deficit (eat fewer calories than you burn)
  • High protein intake (to maintain muscle and curb hunger)
  • Moderate healthy fats
  • Low sugar and refined carbs
  • 4–6 smaller meals per day
  • Stay hydrated (3–4 liters of water daily)

Day 1 Sample Weight Loss Diet Plan

Meal 1 (Breakfast)
  • 3 boiled eggs (or 2 eggs + 2 whites)
  • 1 slice whole grain toast
  • Black coffee or green tea (no sugar)
Meal 2 (Mid-Morning Snack)
  • 1 apple or a handful of berries
  • 5–6 almonds
Meal 3 (Lunch)
  • Grilled chicken breast or tofu
  • 1/2 cup brown rice or quinoa
  • Steamed vegetables (broccoli, spinach, zucchini)
Meal 4 (Snack)
  • Greek yogurt (unsweetened) or 1 boiled egg
  • Green tea
Meal 5 (Dinner)
  • Grilled fish or lentils
  • Mixed vegetable salad with lemon/olive oil
  • 1 small sweet potato (optional)
Meal 6 (Optional – Post-Workout or Late Snack)
  • Protein shake with water or almond milk
