Six Pack Abs Diet Plan

A specialized diet plan designed to help you achieve visible abs by reducing body fat while maintaining muscle mass. This plan focuses on lean proteins, complex carbs, and healthy fats.

Note: This is general guidance — for personalized results, consult a nutritionist or fitness professional.

Key Principles for Six Pack Abs

  • Calorie deficit (300-500 calories below maintenance)
  • High protein intake (1.6-2.2g per kg of bodyweight)
  • Moderate healthy fats (20-30% of total calories)
  • Complex carbohydrates (timed around workouts)
  • 5-6 meals per day
  • Stay hydrated (3-4 liters of water daily)

Day 1 Sample Six Pack Diet Plan

Meal 1 (Breakfast)
  • 6 egg whites + 1 whole egg
  • 1/2 cup oatmeal with berries
  • Green tea or black coffee
Meal 2 (Pre-Workout)
  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 1 banana
  • 5 almonds
Meal 3 (Post-Workout)
  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 1/2 cup brown rice
  • Grilled chicken breast (150g)
Meal 4 (Snack)
  • Greek yogurt with chia seeds
  • Handful of mixed nuts
Meal 5 (Dinner)
  • Grilled fish (200g)
  • Large mixed salad with olive oil
  • 1/2 sweet potato
Meal 6 (Optional – Before Bed)
  • Casein protein shake
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
